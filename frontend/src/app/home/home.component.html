Personal Budget

This is a personal budget application created as a final project for ITIS 5166. To use this application, please do the following:

  1. Create an account and sign in.
  2. Configure categories.
  3. Enter your expenses for each month.
  4. View your analytics and charts on the dashboard.

This application allows you to create categories and track how much you spend each month. It also generates graphs and charts based on your data, allowing you to get a better sense of what your financial situation actually is.
